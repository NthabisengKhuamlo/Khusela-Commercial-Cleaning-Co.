The Airbnb Host Cleaning Checklist for 5-Star Reviews

February 2025  |  6 min read  |  By Khusela Cleaning Co.

Cape Town is one of the world's top Airbnb destinations. With thousands of listings competing for guests, cleanliness is the single biggest factor that separates a 4-star property from a 5-star one. Guests notice everything — and they write about it in reviews.

This is the exact checklist our team uses when turning over Airbnb properties across Cape Town. Use it every single time and your cleanliness score will take care of itself.

Why cleanliness reviews matter so much

Airbnb's algorithm heavily weights cleanliness scores when deciding which listings to show in search results. A property with a 4.6 cleanliness score will consistently rank below one with a 4.9, even if everything else is equal. Over time this compounds — higher rankings mean more bookings, more bookings mean more reviews, more reviews mean higher trust.

One negative cleanliness review can take months to recover from. The cost of a professional clean is a fraction of what a lost booking costs you.

The complete turnover checklist

Bedroom

  • Strip all beds and replace with fresh linen
  • Wipe down headboards, bedside tables and lamps
  • Vacuum mattresses before making beds
  • Dust all surfaces including tops of wardrobes
  • Empty and wipe out drawers if used
  • Check under beds for forgotten items
  • Vacuum carpets or mop floors
  • Clean mirrors and windows
  • Replace any used or missing amenities

Bathroom

  • Scrub toilet inside and out including behind the seat
  • Clean shower and bath, removing all soap scum
  • Descale showerhead and taps
  • Wipe down all tiles and grout
  • Clean mirrors to a streak-free finish
  • Replace toilet paper, soap and shampoo
  • Empty and sanitise the bin
  • Mop the floor and behind the toilet
  • Check drains are clear and flowing

Kitchen

  • Clean inside and outside of all appliances
  • Wipe down all surfaces and splashbacks
  • Clean inside the fridge and remove any leftover food
  • Run the dishwasher or hand wash all dishes
  • Wipe down cupboard doors and handles
  • Clean the stovetop and oven if used
  • Empty, clean and reline the bin
  • Mop the floor
  • Restock basics like coffee, tea and sugar if included

Living areas

  • Vacuum all sofas and cushions
  • Wipe down all hard surfaces and coffee tables
  • Dust all shelves, frames and decor
  • Clean TV screen and remote controls
  • Vacuum carpets or mop hard floors
  • Check all light bulbs are working
  • Ensure remotes have working batteries
  • Wipe down light switches and door handles

The details guests notice most

Beyond the standard clean, it is the small details that guests mention in reviews. Hair in the shower drain. A smudged mirror. A musty smell in the bedroom. Crumbs in a kitchen drawer. These things take seconds to fix but they are what guests remember.

Always do a final walkthrough before you lock up. Stand in the doorway of each room and look at it the way a guest would see it for the first time. Is it inviting? Does it smell fresh? Would you be happy to sleep here tonight?

Managing turnovers efficiently

The biggest challenge for Airbnb hosts is same-day turnovers — a guest checks out at 10am and a new guest arrives at 2pm. That four hour window is tight, especially for larger properties. Professional cleaning teams handle this efficiently because they work systematically through a property in parallel rather than room by room.
